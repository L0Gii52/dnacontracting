Professional EV Charger Installation in Central Valley

As electric vehicles become increasingly popular, having a reliable EV charging station at your home or business is essential. DNA Contracting provides professional EV charger installation services throughout the Central Valley, helping you transition to clean, efficient transportation.

Our licensed C-10 electrical contractor team specializes in installing all types of EV charging stations, from basic Level 1 chargers to high-speed Level 2 charging systems. We work with leading manufacturers to ensure you get the most reliable and efficient charging solution for your needs.

Take advantage of Southern California Edison's EV charger rebate program to significantly reduce your installation costs. As a certified contractor in the SCE program, we can help you navigate the rebate process and maximize your savings while ensuring your installation meets all program requirements.
